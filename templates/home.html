Who we are?

We are a group of like minded graphic artists hiking towards skill development.

Say what? "TENTKOTTA SINCE 2019" and it is fortuitously sprinting till date. Prof Helen from EEE department is leading our club as the Incharge faculty.

We impart not only mere skills but also the ability to think out of the box. Be the best version of yours!



What we do?

The Visual Arts club of TCE, built and running upon the friendship and trust between eachother, we conduct lots of amazing events and gatherings not only to develop our skills but also to have fun.

Our club also facilitate workshops with real time media personalities. We also extend our hand to other clubs for logo, certificate designing and promotion too. As the name goes "TENTKOTTA", we are a family facing the same screen on the same scene!!
